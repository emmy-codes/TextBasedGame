You are stood in a gloomy area of a forest. It is a sunny day, but the thick canopy above means the light can only trickle through in patches that appear to dance across the floor. The air feels thick, moist, and slightly unnerving. No birds are singing. It looks like you are alone, although you sense you are being watched. You can see a Treestump nearby with something leaning against it. There is a dirt path leading North, a very overgrown path to the East, a neat but old cobbled road leading South, and the sound of running water coming from the West.

You gingerly approach the treestump before realising that the object you had spotted was just an Axe that had fallen on its' side. Perhaps this could be useful?

You bend down to retrieve the axe, casually noting that the blade is as sharp as the day it was made, despite being covered in dirt. Brushing the worst of the muck away you carefully place it in your backpack, then look around to see what to do next.
